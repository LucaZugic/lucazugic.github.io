Background

I was a neuroscience student and research assistant.
This has granted me to publish work, win awards, and share my
knowledge and passion through tutoring and mentoring.
During this time I worked in a research lab (SINAPSE Lab)
where I have worked with neuroimaging technologies,
such as EEG and fNIRS, conducted research work, and
developed data focused solutions for the lab and others
in the School of Psychology and Computer Science in
MATLAB, Python, and Julia.
